About Us

Founded in 2024, Dimension 3 is a 3d Printing service that sells you tons of creative items for reasonable prices. We service all of the Tri-State Area and will even ship to you for a small fee. Our products include rotating rings, toy boats, desktop organizers and even clickable fidget toys. What we are most proud of, though, is by far the extendable series. It includes an extendable axe, and extendable katana, and an extendable baton.
